**When:** February 2023

**Where:** Brown University

**Why:** Advanced Computer Graphics project

**How:** `C++`, `Qt`, `Eigen`

</OutlineBox>

<div>

Features:

-   Monte Carlo sampling
-   Indirect and direct illumination with next event estimation
-   Emissive area light sources and soft shadows
-   Four types of BRDFs (diffuse, Phong, mirror, refractive with Fresnel reflection)
-   Tone mapping
-   BRDF importance sampling
-   Depth of field

</div>
